# Landing Page Copy Variations

For future A/B testing on `src/app/page.tsx`, we have developed three variations of the Hero section messaging to test which resonates best with our target audience.

## Variation A: The Aggressive Hook (Current Default)
- **H1:** Your AI stack is bleeding money.
- **Subheadline:** Discover overlapping subscriptions, optimize seat plans, and find cheaper API alternatives. Audit your entire AI spend in under 2 minutes.
- **CTA:** Audit My Spend — Free
- **Why it works:** High emotion, focuses on loss aversion ("bleeding money").

## Variation B: The Analytical Hook
- **H1:** See exactly where your AI budget is going.
- **Subheadline:** Get a deterministic breakdown of your ChatGPT, Copilot, and Claude spend. We find the redundancies so you don't have to.
- **CTA:** Run Free FinOps Audit
- **Why it works:** Professional, appeals to finance and operations leaders who want visibility.

## Variation C: The Benefit-Driven Hook
- **H1:** Cut your AI software costs by 30%.
- **Subheadline:** Join 500+ engineering teams who used our rules engine to right-size their AI subscriptions and eliminate overlapping tools.
- **CTA:** Find My Savings
- **Why it works:** Clear value proposition (30%), adds social proof.
